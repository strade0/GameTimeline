Important: Syncing

To save your progress across devices, you must connect to a room.

1. Open Progress or Notes.

2. Look for the Sync Controls panel (top right).

3. Type any unique text (e.g., "MyUni2025") and click Connect.

* The app will remember this ID for you next time.
